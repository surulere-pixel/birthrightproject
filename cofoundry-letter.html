every institution has a mission. cofoundry provides the venture-building capability to carry it into the world.

we are reaching out because we believe there is a meaningful alignment between what you are building and what cofoundry makes possible — a practical way to stand up accelerators, venture studios, ai adoption programs, innovation labs, and community technology initiatives through proven architecture, experienced operators, and a digital backbone that holds it together.

cofoundry is a venture infrastructure platform built by the birthright project, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit operating across africa, latin america, the caribbean, and the diaspora. every program we run is powered by three assets: blueprints (proven models), boards (experienced operators and global teams), and backbone (engineering, design, and ai systems).

this is an invitation to explore what a cofoundry-powered program could look like for your institution — whether that is creating new mission-driven ventures or helping communities adopt useful technologies at scale.

the pages that follow outline the platform, the programs, and the reach. if anything resonates, we would welcome the conversation.

cofoundry — a birthright venture
01
how it works
cofounding teams carry ventures further. cofoundry matches every venture with the right team, the right model, and the right tools — so every founder starts with company and a roadmap.

proven blueprints

business models, business plans, and program architectures drawn from ventures already in motion — adapted to your mission. what took years to learn is available on day one.

matched teams

deep market knowledge, experienced operators, and a technical + ai bench matched to every venture. we co-found alongside you, hands on the work. sponsor organizations also gain early leadership development for their mid-level staff through board seats and startup mentorship roles.

speed + reuse

with the full stack — blueprints, tools, teams, and infrastructure — a venture can launch as a distributed multinational brand in as little as one month. experiments that deliver learnings become reusable assets for the next class, carried forward with every cohort. libraries and blueprints stay open and free to program alumni and staff of sponsor organizations — the value compounds long after the cohort ends.

the result: overall success rates go up — because every venture builds on what came before, with a team that's been matched to the mission.

the platform
three assets carry the work and allow programs to move from idea to launch.

proven program models, curriculum, venture templates, cohort structures, diagnostics, and handover frameworks — the reusable architecture behind every cofoundry deployment.

backbone

why this matters: cofoundry brings execution capacity — operators who build alongside you, with the tools and teams to carry a program from brief to launch.
